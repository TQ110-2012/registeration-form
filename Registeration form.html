Codingal is a company that teaches kids how to code. Founded by Vivek Prakash and Satyam Bharanwal, its goal is to make coding fun and easy for everyone. They offer online classes, competitions, and a friendly community to help students learn important skills for the future. Codingal has grown quickly and is known for its engaging approach to education.


Sep 2020: Founding team starts building Codingal and launch Beta version.
Jan 2021: Codingal is launched in India for all students.
Jan 2021: Codingal gets funded by Y Combinator in W21 batch.
Apr 2021: Codingal is funded by Rebright Partners, GSF, and Java Capital.
Sep 2021: Codingal raises over USD 1 Million in a seed funding from reputed investors.
Jan 2023: 500,000 students registered on Codingal till date, grows revenue 10x in 2022.

Codingal is a great way to learn coding. It has a clear plan that helps you start as a beginner and advance your skills. You get to do fun exercises and projects, and there are helpful instructors available. You can connect with other learners and choose from languages like Python and Java. Codingal focuses on real-world projects, making it easy to apply what you learn. Overall, it's a simple and flexible way to learn coding.
Kids learn in Codingal through fun and interactive lessons. They take part in hands-on exercises and work on exciting projects, like building games. Many courses include live classes with teachers who help answer questions and give feedback. The learning process feels like a game, which keeps kids motivated. They can also collaborate with classmates on projects and learn at their own pace. This mix of activities makes learning coding enjoyable and effective for kids.
